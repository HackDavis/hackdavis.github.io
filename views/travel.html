TRAVEL REIMBURSEMENT
We'd love to have hackers outside of UC Davis join us here at HackDavis, and we want to do everything we can to make that happen. Travel reimbursements (maximum of $50) will be handed out on a first-come first-serve basis to hackers who are admitted outside of UC Davis.

To secure your reimbursement, first confirm your attendence to the event on the typeform link in your acceptance email. Only the first 40 individuals who confirm will secure reimbursements. After submitting a project at HackDavis, send an email to hello@hackdavis.io with the following:

  • your full name
  • devpost submission link
  • mailing address
  • an attachment of your itinerary/gas receipt to and from the event

We will then mail out your reimbursement following the event. Email us if you have any further doubts. We look forward to seeing you there!
