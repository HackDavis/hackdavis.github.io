Prompt 1: One thing that Gamers Gift would love to have is a mobile app that allows volunteering and participating in Gamers Gift events much easier, ideally users would be able to schedule themselves to volunteer, view opportunities, receive ETA based on their location, and easily check-in and share their participated. Any features that would make volunteering easier and more enjoyable! Likewise, we have different types of events that may need to be separated (digital vs local).

Prompt 2: Another thing that we be of great value is a system similar to Charity Miles, wherin individuals can earn money for Gamers Gift just by gaming and viewing/listening to sponsors. We believe the system may work best if implemented with Hour Tracking functions of popular gaming services (Steam, Battle.net, Origin). Alternatively, money could be earned for Gamers Gift by earning achievements in game.

Prompt 3: During our visits, some patients like to experience Real-World locations in Virtual Reality. Sometimes an elderly women in the hospital would like to visit Paris, or a young-boy visit a skate park. Currently, there is no simplified way to enter Google Maps street view through a VR system while being able to control movement. We’d like to be able to offer patients the ability to move around Google maps in VR, through any control method.

Prompt 4: For users of Twitch, a popular livestreaming service among gamers, donations to the livestreamer are common. We’d like to offer a payment solution wherein donors on Twitch can allocate a certain amount of their donation between the livestreamer, with an automatic setting of 5% going to Gamers Gift.
