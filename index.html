What is HackDavis?

HackDavis is the first major collegiate hackathon at UC Davis completely run by students. In Spring 2016, 400+ students developers, designers, and creatives will come together for 36 hours of building software and hardware solutions for social good. We’re providing a platform to bring together the most talented students in California to address the world’s most pressing social issues for three specific areas: Health & Wellness, Education, and Environment.

Why social good?

We want people to build projects with a meaningful impact and hope to foster a community dedicated to social change. We find that most hackathons often don’t result with projects as practical solutions to specific societal problems and we’re looking to change that. With every 50 projects submitted by participants at HackDavis, we will be donating $1000 to a charity of choice.
