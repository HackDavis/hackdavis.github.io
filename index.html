Stories from HackDavis 2016

Rohit Tigga
Co-founder, UCD '17





Starting HackDavis was one of the best decisions I made in my life. I'm glad we got to create a hackathon that was entirely SOCIAL GOOD focused. We had tons of students participate who had never coded before, and almost had a 50:50 gender ratio.

Misha Cristobal
Director of External Affairs, UCD '17





It's just so crazy thinking about how we woke up on May 7th and made history. But we didn't just make UC Davis' first major collegiate hackathon, we dedicated hundreds of hackers' talents to social good. I know we'll just keep getting better and better, and I'm super stoked to show a bigger, better HackDavis 2017.

Cindy Chin
Westfield Labs Sponsor Representative





With a turnout of more than 300 students, 50+ projects submitted and $6,000 going towards charities such as Re-Volv, Teach for America and New Story Inc, HackDavis 2016 was a huge success! We were extremely impressed by the quality of the projects and can't wait for next year's HackDavis!
